## Rationale

Cool grays with a lavender undertone. Warmer than corporate blue-gray, cooler than beige. The lavender whisper gives it a human quality without feeling decorative.

Arabic calligraphy mark will influence future palette revision — the mark should drive the color, not the other way around.
